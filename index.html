FAQ

How much does Shipper cost?

Shipper is free to use for doing cost comparisons between USPS, UPS, and FedEx. The $14.99 "Shipper Pro" features allow you to actually print shipping labels through the app.

How much does postage cost?

You pay the carrier rate plus $0.05 per label. This 5 cents goes to EasyPost, our pricing and label creation partner.

Why do I only see USPS rates?

After you have created your EasyPost account you must add each carrier account that you wish to utilize. Add your carrier accounts on the EasyPost carriers page.

How do I add my FedEx account?

Unfortunately, FedEx does not make it easy to use their services in an app. Fortunately, the good people at EasyPost have some material available to help guide you through the process.

What is Shipper?

Shipper is your modern alternative to the big players like Stamps.com and Endicia. We believe that we can provide you with a great product for managing your shipping needs without charging you high, ongoing fees.

Shipper is the app that allows you to compare shipping rates between multiple carriers and quickly purchase and print labels.

How do I get started?

  1. Install Shipper from the Windows Store.
  2. Create an account at EasyPost.
  3. Add your preferred carriers to EasyPost (USPS comes standard).
  4. Start saving time and money!
